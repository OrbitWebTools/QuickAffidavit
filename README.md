# 📜 QuickAffidavit | Free Gap Certificate Generator format

**[Live Demo / Use Tool Here](https://orbitwebtools.github.io/QuickAffidavit/)**

## 🚀 Overview
**QuickAffidavit** is a 100% free, browser-based utility designed to help Indian students quickly generate legally formatted **Gap Certificates (Gap Affidavits)** for college and university admissions. It perfectly aligns text for printing on ₹50/₹100 Non-Judicial E-Stamp papers.

## ✨ Key Features
* **E-Stamp Ready:** Automatically leaves a 3.5-inch top margin, ensuring the text does not print over the official E-Stamp.
* **Auto Age Calculator:** Simply enter your Date of Birth, and the tool calculates your exact age for the legal format.
* **Cyber Cafe Pro Mode:** Designed for CSC centers and Notary agents. Save your shop details locally to auto-fill for every new customer.
* **100% Free & Private:** No watermarks, no server uploads, and no sign-ups. Everything runs securely and locally in your browser.

## 🛠️ How to Use
1. Open the [QuickAffidavit Web App](https://orbitwebtools.github.io/QuickAffidavit/).
2. Fill in the student's details (Name, Father's Name, Address, Gap Years, and valid Reason).
3. Toggle the **"Print on E-Stamp Paper"** checkbox if you are printing directly onto an official stamp paper.
4. Click **Print Certificate / Save PDF** to generate the document.
5. Sign the printed document in front of a registered Notary Public or Oath Commissioner.
