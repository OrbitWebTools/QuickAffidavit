📝 What is a Gap Certificate for College Admission?

A Gap Certificate (also known as a Gap Affidavit) is a mandatory legal self-declaration document required by universities and colleges in India. If a student has taken a drop year or a break from their regular studies for preparation (like NEET, JEE, UPSC), health issues, or financial reasons, they must submit this affidavit on a Non-Judicial Stamp Paper to prove they were not involved in any illegal activities during the gap period.

Yes! After printing the document, you must sign it in front of a registered Notary Public or Oath Commissioner and get their official seal/stamp to make it legally valid for university admissions.
